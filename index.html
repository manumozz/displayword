Features

Everything your operator needs

Built around how worship services actually run — not how software companies think they do.

🖥️

Three Screens at Once

Independently control the Hall, Stage, and Preacher displays from a single operator console. Each screen shows exactly what it should.

🎼

Lyrics + Chords

Show clean lyrics to the congregation while musicians see chords in their key — with one-click transposition to any key on the fly.

⚡

Special Cues for Musicians

Send instant signals to the stage screen: A Cappella, Drums Only, Repeat, Slower — as brief banners the hall never sees.

📱

Works with Any Equipment

One PC and one projector? That's enough. In the Team edition, musicians use their phones as stage screens over Wi-Fi.

📚

Song Library

Organize all your congregation's songs with full editing, verse ordering, and key detection. Build a setlist in under 5 minutes.

🌐

Works Offline

No internet required during services. Everything runs locally on your PC. Reliable when you need it most.

How it works

Simple for operators, powerful for services

01

Build your setlist

Pick songs from your library, set keys, arrange the order. Takes 5 minutes before service.

02

Run the service

Click through verses and choruses. Each screen updates instantly. Send cues to musicians with one tap.

03

Never lose the flow

Blackout, quick messages, key changes mid-song — everything you need without breaking the moment.

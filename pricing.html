Frequently asked questions
Is the free version really free?
Yes, completely. DisplayWord Free has no trial period, no expiry, no ads, and no credit card required. It includes the Hall screen and full song library — suitable for many smaller churches and house groups.
Is this a subscription? Do I pay every month or year?
No subscriptions. Church and Connect editions are a single one-time payment. You own the software and receive all future updates at no extra charge.
What does "web screens via Wi-Fi" mean in Connect?
In Connect, DisplayWord acts as a local server. Musicians open a URL on their phone or tablet and see their own custom screen — chords, cues, key — in a regular browser. No app installation needed. Everything stays on your local network; no internet required.
Can I upgrade from Free to Church later?
Yes. When you're ready, purchase a Church or Connect license — your songs, song books, and settings all carry over. You don't start from scratch.
What operating systems are supported?
DisplayWord runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11. It requires .NET 6 (installed automatically if missing). Mac and Linux versions are not planned at this time.
My church can't afford even $29. Is there help available?
Yes. If your church is in a low-income country or has genuine financial need, reach out to us at hello@displayword.com. We want DisplayWord to serve every church, regardless of resources.
